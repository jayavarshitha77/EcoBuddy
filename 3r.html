🌍 Reduce, Reuse, Recycle ♻️

Reduce 📉

Reduce Tips ✅ 🚫

  • 🚫 Say No to Single-Use Plastics – Use reusable bags, bottles, and straws instead! 🛍️
  • 🥗 Reduce Food Waste – Plan meals, store leftovers properly, and compost scraps. 🍎
  • 💡 Save Energy – Use LED bulbs, unplug devices when not in use, and opt for solar power. ☀️
  • 🖨️ Print Less – Go digital when possible and print on both sides of the paper. 📄
  • 📦 Minimize Packaging – Buy in bulk and choose products with recyclable packaging. 📦
  • 🚿 Save Water – Take shorter showers, fix leaks, and turn off taps while brushing. 🚰
  • 🚲 Choose Green Transport – Walk, bike, carpool, or take public transport to reduce emissions. 🚆
  • 🌿 Support Sustainable Brands – Buy from eco-friendly companies that prioritize sustainability. 🛒
  • 🏠 Use Smart Home Tech – Invest in energy-efficient appliances and smart thermostats. 🔋

Reuse 🔄

Reuse Tips ✅

  • ✅ Repurpose old clothes – Turn old t-shirts into cleaning rags or DIY tote bags.
  • ✅ Use glass jars – Store spices, leftovers, or even small hardware items like screws.
  • ✅ Refill water bottles – Instead of buying new plastic bottles, invest in a reusable one.
  • ✅ Repurpose furniture – Give old furniture a fresh look with paint or new upholstery.
  • ✅ Reuse gift wrap – Save wrapping paper, ribbons, and gift bags for future use.
  • ✅ Donate or swap items – Clothes, books, and electronics can find a second home instead of being trashed.
  • ✅ Turn paper waste into scratch pads – Cut unused sections of printed paper for notes and reminders.
  • ✅ Refill ink cartridges – Instead of buying new ones, get them refilled at a print shop.
  • ✅ Save plastic containers – Use yogurt cups, butter tubs, and other plastic containers for storage.

Recycle ♻️

General Recycling Tips ♻️

  • ♻️ Know Your Local Rules – Different areas have different recycling guidelines. Check your city's website.
  • ♻️ Rinse Before Recycling – Clean food containers before tossing them in the bin to avoid contamination.
  • ♻️ Separate Materials – Sort plastics, glass, paper, and metal properly to make recycling efficient.

Paper & Cardboard 📄

  • 📄 Flatten Boxes – Saves space and makes it easier to recycle.
  • 📄 Avoid Shredding Paper – Shredded paper is harder to recycle.
  • 📄 Say No to Greasy Pizza Boxes – Only the clean parts of cardboard are recyclable.

Plastic 🚫✅🔄

  • 🚫 Reduce Plastic Use – Choose reusable bags, bottles, and containers.
  • ✅ Check Recycling Symbols – Only certain types of plastic are accepted in curbside bins.
  • 🔄 Reuse When Possible – Turn old plastic containers into storage organizers.

Glass & Metal 🍾🥫🔄

  • 🍾 Recycle Glass Bottles & Jars – They can be endlessly recycled without quality loss.
  • 🥫 Remove Metal Lids – Separate them before recycling for proper processing.
  • 🔄 Scrap Metal Can Be Recycled – Take old appliances, tools, and wires to a scrap metal facility.
